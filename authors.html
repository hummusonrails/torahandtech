Ben Greenberg

Software Developer & Rabbi

Ben Greenberg is a software developer and rabbi who brings a unique perspective to the intersection of ancient wisdom and modern technology. As co-author of Torah && Tech, he explores how millennia of Jewish thought can inform and enrich our relationship with technology.

Yechiel Kalmenson

Software Developer & Author

Yechiel Kalmenson is a software developer and author who co-created the Torah && Tech newsletter and book. He brings together his passion for coding and Jewish learning to spark meaningful conversations about how we engage with the digital world.
