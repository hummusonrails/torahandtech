Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Torah && Tech

What is Torah && Tech about?

Torah && Tech brings more than three millennia of Jewish insights and wisdom to bear on technology topics including artificial intelligence, code ownership, object-oriented design, social media ethics, and more. It explores what Jewish ethics and wisdom have to say about modern technology, and what innovations in technology mean for our interpersonal lives at work, with our families, and with our communities.

Who wrote Torah && Tech?

Torah && Tech was written by Ben Greenberg, a software developer and rabbi, and Yechiel Kalmenson, a software developer and rabbi. The book grew out of a weekly newsletter that has been running for more than a year.

Where can I buy the book?

Torah && Tech is available on Amazon in both paperback and Kindle e-book formats.

Is there an e-book version?

Yes! Torah && Tech is available as a Kindle e-book on Amazon.

Can I order internationally?

Yes. For international paperback orders, you can purchase Torah && Tech through Book Depository, which offers worldwide shipping. The Kindle e-book is also available internationally on Amazon.
