About The Book

Torah to Tech

What does Jewish ethics and wisdom have to say on topics such as artificial intelligence, code ownership, object oriented design, and more? Torah && Tech brings more than three millennia of insights and wisdom to bear on technology.

Tech to Torah

What does the innovations in technology mean for our inter-personal lives at work, with our family and with our communities? Does social media change the way we ought to interact with others? Am I my avatar's keeper? Torah && Tech brings tech into the conversation with Torah.

It's a Conversation

Torah && Tech does not aim to talk to you, but rather talk with you. It is the beginning of a conversation. The book grew out of a weekly newsletter running now for more than a year. It is a dialectic between the text and the people, not a monologue.
